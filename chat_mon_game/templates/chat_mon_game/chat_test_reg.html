Test Instructions

Read the below instructions carefully before the test. You can access this information anytime during the test by clicking on the Help button (The timer will not stop running during this period). Do not REFRESH the page anytime during the exam period.
  • 1. Log in with your Name and Email address
  • 2. A timer will start soon after you log in to the system
  • 3. On the test page, there will be a chat segment with messages populating every 3 seconds.
  • 4. There will be normal messages and also messages with negative contents. Candidates are required to identify only negative messages from the chat and label their severity, meaning not all messages necessarily need labellingnot all messages necessarily need labelling.
  • 5. Clicking on any individual chat will display the message on the bottom pane where the candidates can mark their responses.
  • 6. The negative messages are categorized into three, namely Offensive, Suspicious or Moderate.
  • 7. Selected message will be highlighted in blue in the chat window.
  • 8. Once you give a response, it will disappear from the bottom pane and will be highlighted in green in the chat window.
  • 9. If you feel nothing wrong with the selected message, you can click on the No Action option to clear your response.
  • 10. You can go back and change your responses anytime during the test period. (Click the message â®• Change your response)
  • 11. The test will submit automatically after the time period.
  • 12. Do not REFRESH the page anytime during the exam period.
